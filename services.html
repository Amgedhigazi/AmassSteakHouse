Corporate Events

Business Cocktail Receptions

It's about business. Follow the brief guidelines below and you will find yourself moving with confidence from one excellent business opportunity to the next at your next business event.

Hold your drink or food in your left hand. Your right hand must be clean, dry, and available for shaking hands. There is no gracious way to juggle cocktail party food — the foods served at these events are called hors d'oeuvres or canapés.

They may be served on a cracker, a miniature piece of toast, or on a little piece of bread. Hors d'oeuvres may be eaten with fingers or a utensil.

Celebrations

Social & Family Events

Birthday party bookings are first come, first served. All party options have a limit for participant capacity. All parties require the renter to supply their own food and decorations.

Party Packages

For all party packages, payment must be completed a minimum of seven days prior to the party date. Room time includes set up time and clean-up time by the renter. Parental supervision is required for all birthday parties and the exact number of participants must be identified when booking your reservation.

Arena Party

Your arena party booking includes 50 minutes of ice time, followed by one hour in the community room for gifts and food. All skating admission standards for public skating apply during the skate. Maximum number of people: 25.

Outdoor & Pool Party

Booking includes one hour of pool time with a certified lifeguard, followed by one hour in the community room for gifts and food. All swim admission standards apply. This includes one adult in the water for every three children aged 9 and under. Maximum: 25 people.
